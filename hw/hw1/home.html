Viruses

Viruses are a type of malware that can cause harm to your computer. They can be spread through email attachments, infected websites, and other means. Viruses can cause a variety of problems, including slowing down your computer, stealing your personal information, and damaging your files. It is important to protect your computer from viruses by using antivirus software and being cautious when downloading files or clicking on links.
