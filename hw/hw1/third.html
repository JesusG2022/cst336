Here is some viruses you should know.

1) Browser Hijacker

  The first virus is called a browser hijacker. It is when someone is controlling your web browser and unwanted websites appear. It is not as dangerous as the other viruses, but it can be annoying. If this happen do not download anything from those website.

2) Trojan Horses

  The next virus is trojan horses and one of most dangerous viruses. It was name base on the wooden horse that was use in the Trojan War. This virus is commonly use in cyber-crime like scammers, and other related crimes. This virus look like as a normal program but when it install. It have access to the computers files and private information. So private information example are bank accounts, home address, and other presonal information.

3) Overwrite Virus

  The last virus is overwrite virus. This virus is the most dangerous virus because it can delete all the files on the computer. Once the data or files are change or delete, it is impossible to recover the files. The only way not destroy the computers is to have a backup of the files. But if you do not have a backup the whole coputer will render useless.
