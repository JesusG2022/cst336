Be Careful









Viruses can hide anywhere. For example:






Email Attachments













Malware(USB or CDs)










Website or Internet










Try to avoid it

To avoid this common tropes, always be cautious when opening email attachments. If you do not know the email do not open it. Be careful when using external storage devices. If you see a USB or CD on the ground, do not pick it up. Or when buying a USB or CD, just make sure where you buy it from (Do not buy used USB). Be careful when visiting websites. Do not download from non-secure websites.
